What we do

Welcome to Custom Love, where we weave heartfelt stories into every stitch. Our cozy home-based studio is dedicated to crafting enchanting baby blankets that reflect the essence of your unique love. At Custom Love, we believe in the power of personalization, turning ordinary blankets into extraordinary expressions of affection. Our skilled artisans delicately embroider each piece, adding a touch of magic to create timeless keepsakes for your little ones. Whether it's a name, a special date, or a whimsical design, we take pride in translating your emotions into intricately woven threads. Wrap your bundle of joy in the warmth of Custom Love's handmade blankets, where every creation is a celebration of the love that makes your family one-of-a-kind.

About me

Meet Sarah, the heart and soul behind Custom Love. A devoted mother with a passion for creating meaningful moments, Sarah embarked on a heartfelt journey to bring warmth and personalization into the lives of families. Inspired by the joy of welcoming her own little ones into the world. Driven by a desire to intertwine love and creativity, Sarah envisioned a space where every baby blanket could be a canvas for cherished memories. Join Sarah on this enchanting journey, where every embroidered thread tells a story of a mother's devotion and a family's love.
